Brussels’ number 1 international rugby club!

Welcome to the BBRFC Celtic, Brussels' most international club, located in the heart of the city and with a history dating back to 1968! We have three full Men's XVs squads, a Women’s XV, a mixed Touch team, and our own Rugby School for all ages from U6 to U19.

With a great mix of international and Belgian members, superb pitch and club-house centrally located in Brussels!

If you are new to town, or have been here for a while, and want to play the most competitive expat rugby in Brussels, we are the club for you!
