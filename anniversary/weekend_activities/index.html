Touch

A touch tournament will take place from 10.00 till 12.00 on Saturday 30th June.

Where

All are welcome to play from rugby school players, to parents, to BBRFC Celtic players (male and female) past and present. This tournament will take place at our home pitch

Registration

Touch Tournament registration is mandatory and available here BBRFC Celtic Touch Tournament Registration

Ladies

Our Ladies XV will play a match against an International XV ladies team.

Where

The match will be from 12:30 till 13:30 on Saturday 30th of June and will take place at our home pitch.

Old Boys

Our 1st XV will play a match against and Old Boys XV.

Where

The match will be from 14.00 till 15.00 on Saturday 30th of June and will take place at our home pitch. All Old Boys must register to play!

Registration
