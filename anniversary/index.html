About

The 50th and 20th anniversary of the BBRFC Celtic Rugby Club!

Once a BC, always a BC !

BBRFC-Celtic goes Royal as its BBRFC lineage celebrates its 50th anniversary.

BBRFC-Celtic goes China as its Celtic lineage celebrates its 20th anniversary.

BBRFC-Celtic goes Platinum as, together, it has combined for 70 years of the most internationally flavo(u)red rugby in Belgium.

Come one, come all, it would be a tragedy to miss it!

We hope you are royally ready for the event of the half-century!

When

SAVE THE DATE !

Saturday, 30 June 2018

Where

Hôtel de la Poste, Brussels

Who

All are welcome, Barbarians and Celts alike, ol' boys and fresh faces, from the founding mothers and fathers, to the parents & kids of the rugby school (just make sure you're over 16!)

Price

€110 full, €90 concessions

This ticket price includes:
  • Champagne & canapés
  • Three course luxury meal with free-flowing drinks
  • Open bar all night long
  • Live entertainment
  • Multimedia entertainment
  • Grand, central location
  • Music and dancing
  • A chance to make a weekend of it, to meet new people, to reunite with old teammates, and be a part of an historic moment in the club's history!
