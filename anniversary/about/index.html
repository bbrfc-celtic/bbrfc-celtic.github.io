Welcome to the 50th & 20th anniversary of the BBRFC Celtic!

Once a BC, always a BC!

BBRFC-Celtic goes Royal as its BBRFC lineage celebrates its 50th anniversary.

BBRFC-Celtic goes China as its Celtic lineage celebrates its 20th anniversary.

BBRFC-Celtic goes Platinum as, together, it has combined for 70 years of the most internationally flavo(u)red rugby in Belgium.

Come one, come all, it would be a tragedy to miss it!

We hope you are royally ready for the event of the half-century!

We have a fun-packed agenda in store for you starting on Friday 29th June and running through to the early hours of Sunday 1st July!!!!

Please see the agenda below and register for the various activities that take place over Friday 29/06/2018 and Saturday 30/06/2018 ASAP!
