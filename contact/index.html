Pitch and Clubhouse

Ruck 'n Maul

Our club house - Ruck 'n Maul - is located at Triomflaan 52, 1160 Auderghem right across the street. There you will find live sports (rugby), good food, free wifi, a pool table, cheap beer & a friendly atmosphere. It is run by rugby players for rugby & sports enthusiasts.
