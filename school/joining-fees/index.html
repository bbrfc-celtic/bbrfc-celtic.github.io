Joining

If you would like to give us a try just send us an email to rugbyschool@bbrfc-celtic.be so that we can confirm possible trial dates that might suit you. All you need to try out is a pair of rugby boots or trainers, shorts and a T-Shirt or jersey. Note that because we play on a high tech artificial pitch only plastic studs may be used (no metal studs).

The first three training sessions are free, so why not drop in & give us a go? Chances are you'll be back...

Registration

In order to register you need to send us a scanned copy of the medical form (see above) and an email (at rugbyschool@bbrfc-celtic.be) with the following information:

  • First name
  • Family name
  • Gender
  • Nationality
  • Date and place of birth
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Confirmation of fee payment

Medical Form

Payment policy!

We have a strict policy regarding the fee payment. No player is to play or be registered until the fee has been paid. The latter is considered outstanding if not settled by the end of September.

Payment plans are always an option, so please contact our treasurer if you would like one.
